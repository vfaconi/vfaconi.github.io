Site Purpose

It is each day more challenging discovery how will be the weather when starting the day, this has many reasons and one of them is climate change. It is very common to find people that are looking for this information before going out, or when they are planning a travel to the beach, so the purpose of this site is to provide reliable information about the weather, but not only about the weather, we are a community site, so we will publish news, events, local companies advertisement and other information that is relevant for our region.
