Purpose

Our purpose is to help the local commerce increase in a sustainable way and contribute to the city increase too. Through training, news, and events we want to capacitate our entrepreneurs and provide support during these tough times.

Goal

Our goal is for every local commerce to find help and support to adapt their business to the current times, through consultancy and guidance to change, when is possible, from presential in a technological business. This way we want our entrepreneurs and city continuing increase despite the crises.
