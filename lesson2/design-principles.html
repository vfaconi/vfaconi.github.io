Design Principles

  • Repetition
  • Animal Equality

Description:The repetition is in the font type of the menu titles, in the icon color, and in the own icon. The color also is the same on the call for action button that contributes to the site consistency.

  • Visual Hierarchy
  • Amazon

Description:The Amazon website uses the visual hierarchy to show the most important things on its website. The first is the images that show the products on sale and the amazon products (the images changing while you navigate through the website), those images are bigger and have different colors from the rest of the page. Then you see the login button that has other color and is easy to find. There is an image showing the January sales with other color and size and the search field is on the top of the page, that facilitates the user to find what she/he wants.

  • Contrast
  • M&S

Description:The M&S website uses the contrast to highlight the January sales with the red on the background color and words with white color. The words "the sale" are bigger than others in the same space.
